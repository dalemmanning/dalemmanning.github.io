About Me

Other than the stuff in the sidebar, what can I say about myself...

I am Curriculum Leader Media Studies at a secondary school on the south coast of England. As well as Media Studies, I also teach Citizenship and Music to year 8, 10 and 11.

While my main focus is now learning the role of curriculum leader, I also still occasionally serve as the Digital Media Manager for the school helping to drive the schools ICT policies and use of technology.

In September 2013 I completed my Open University Open Degree achieving a 2.2 with honours.

I started this blog while undergoing CBT therapy in order to have an outlet for views and creative side. Most importantly I wanted to start writing for myself rather than work or study.
